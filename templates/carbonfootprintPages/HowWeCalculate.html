How We Calculate

Transport

By asking the user the mode of travel to school/work, the distance and frequency travelled, users can gauge the differences in emissions based on how they choose to travel

Food

Food emissions are calculated based on the amount of different types of foods purchased per week. These values are then applied over the course of the year.

Overnight

By asking users questions related to conscious decisions they make when they go to sleep, users can calculate the differences in potential lifestyle changes over the course of the year.
